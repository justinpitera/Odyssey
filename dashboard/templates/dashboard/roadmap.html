Product Roadmap

Our journey towards improving and expanding the flight simulator experience.

  • March 2024 - Launch Phase

    Simtrail launch introduction of basic flight tracking features via the Simtrail, VATSIM, and IVAO networks.

  • April 2024 - Remote Control Expansion

    Introducing advanced remote control features: Pause, toggle pause during descent, and adjust heading, speed, and altitude from anywhere. Enjoy seamless control for an elevated flight experience.

  • Q3 2024 - Competition and Engagement

    Introduce leaderboards, competitions, and more interactive elements.

  • Further Updates

    Simulated market experience, implementing features requested by the community, enhancing user experience.
